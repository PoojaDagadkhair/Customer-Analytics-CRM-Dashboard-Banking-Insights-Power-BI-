# Bank CRM Perfomance Dashboard ( Power BI) 

This project analyzes customer data from a bank to understand customer behavior, churn patterns, credit score segmentation, product usage, and regional trends. SQL and Power BI were used for data analysis, visualization, and deriving insights.

## Dataset Summary

The dataset contains the following tables:  
- **Bank_Churn:** Customer account details and churn information  
- **CustomerInfo:** Customer personal info and salary  
- **CreditCard:** Credit card ownership  
- **ActiveCustomer:** Active/Inactive members  
- **ExitCustomer:** Exit category (Exit/Retain)  
- **Gender:** Gender info  
- **Geography:** Geographic location  

**Key Points:**  
- 3 main regions: France, Spain, Germany  
- Customers aged 18+ with varying tenure and product usage  
- Credit score categories: Poor, Fair, Good, Very Good, Excellent  

---

## Key Insights

- **Customer Churn:** Higher for customers with low credit scores or no credit card.  
- **Active Customers:** France has the highest number of active long-term customers.  
- **Product Usage:** Majority use 1–2 products; cross-selling opportunities exist.  
- **Salary & Balance:** No direct correlation found with churn.  
- **Tenure Patterns:** Customers with 3–5 years tenure spend more, suggesting engagement potential.  
- **Demographics:** Younger customers (0–40) hold higher balances but have higher risk of inactivity.  

---
## Tools Used

- **SQL**: For data querying, aggregation, and preparing insights.  
- **Power BI**: For dashboard creation, visualization, and KPI tracking.  
- **Excel / Data Cleaning**: For handling missing values, data validation, and preprocessing.  

---

## Dashboard Features

- **Key KPIs**:  
  - Customer Exit Rate  
  - Account Balance  
  - Customer Tenure  
  - Credit Score  
  - Product Usage  

- **Slicers**:  
  - Year  
  - Region  
  - Gender  
  - Active Status  

- **Conditional Formatting**:  
  - Highlights customers at risk of churn based on account balance and activity  

---

## Conclusion

This project helps the bank to:  

1. Identify high-risk customers.  
2. Understand customer behavior and spending patterns.  
3. Develop targeted engagement and cross-selling strategies.  
4. Improve customer retention across regions and demographics.
